Steamed Egg

Description

Chinese steamed eggs or water egg is a traditional Chinese dish found all over China. Eggs are beaten to a consistency similar to that used for an omelette and then steamed.

Ingredients

  • 4 egg
  • 2 cup warm water
  • 2 stalk scallions
  • 0.50 tbsp salt
  • 0.50 tbsp chicken bouillon
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 dash white pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cut scallions
  2. Boil water in the wok
  3. Start mixing eggs
  4. Prepare warm water and mix with eggs
  5. Start steaming eggs
  6. Boil, simmer, wait
  7. Add garnishes and seasonings
  8. Remove bowl from wok
